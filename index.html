SelfPaced.io is an innovative self-paced learning platform dedicated to empowering individuals with the necessary tech skills to achieve their career goals. We go beyond the traditional bootcamp model, providing a flexible and personalized learning experience that allows you to acquire the skills you need at your own pace and on your own terms. What sets us apart from universities and bootcamps is our revolutionary approach to learning and unwavering commitment to making a positive impact on society.

At SelfPaced, we curate the best content to help students learn the most in-demand development skills. Our students learn using comprehensive yet bite-sized content, ensuring a deep understanding of the material before moving forward. Our content is designed to be accessible to all, regardless of background or prior experience. We also provide a supportive community of mentors and peers to help students stay motivated and on track.

Our platform leverages AI/ChatGPT as a part of the learning process, enabling faster assistance and helping students figure out solutions instead of simply providing answers. We also utilize reinforcement learning through flashcards, and unlike most bootcamps that focus solely on frontend/backend development, our curriculum covers comprehensive topics such as data structures, design patterns, and architectural patterns. Our platform also includes a job board, resume builder, and interview prep to help students land their dream job.

We prioritize assisting individuals affected by the criminal justice system, the unemployed, and the underemployed. We are thrilled to announce the forthcoming launch of SelfPaced.io in 2023, as we continue our mission to transform lives through accessible and inclusive education.
